# remotion-mcp Cookbooks

Worked examples showing complete, real-world workflows using `remotion-mcp`.  
Each cookbook shows the **exact prompts**, the **tool calls they trigger**, and the **expected output**.

---

## Available Cookbooks

| # | Cookbook | What You Build | Time |
|---|----------|---------------|------|
| [01](./01-youtube-intro/) | YouTube Channel Intro | Animated title card MP4 | ~5 min |
| [02](./02-data-driven-video/) | Data-Driven Sales Recap | Parameterized video via `inputProps` | ~8 min |
| [03](./03-iterate-with-ai/) | Edit & Iterate Loop | Read → modify → preview cycle | ~5 min |

> 🎬 Cookbooks **01** and **02** include full **video walkthroughs** — watch the entire workflow from prompt to rendered output.

---

## How to Read These

Each cookbook uses this format:

> 💬 **Prompt** — what you type to your AI assistant  
> 🔧 **Tool called** — what `remotion-mcp` executes under the hood  
> ✅ **Result** — what comes back

You don't need to understand the tools to follow along — just copy the prompts.

---

## Prerequisites

- `remotion-mcp` connected to your AI editor ([setup guide](../GETTING_STARTED.md))
- Node.js ≥ 18 and Google Chrome installed
- ~500MB free disk space for Remotion dependencies
